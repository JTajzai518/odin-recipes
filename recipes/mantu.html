My Favorite Mantu Recipe

Description

This recipe is one of the most popular! Afghans love mantu, and after you make this delicious dumpling, it'll become one of your favorite dishes as well!

Ingredients

  • Wrapper
  • Ground beef
  • Cilantro
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Let the wrappers defrost.
  2. Make the ground beef with Afghan seasoning.
  3. Cut the onions and cook them.
  4. Mix onions with the ground beef a stuff wrappers.
  5. Put cilantro on top and enjoy!
